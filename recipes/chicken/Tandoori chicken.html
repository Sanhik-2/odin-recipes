  • Butter Chicken

    • courtesy:Prabal Pratap Singh

    Butter Chicken is a popular Indian dish made with tender chicken pieces cooked in a rich, creamy tomato-based sauce. It's often served with naan or rice and is known for its delicious blend of spices and buttery texture.

    Here's the recipe:

    Ingredients:

    1. 500g boneless chicken, cut into pieces
    2. 1 cup yogurt
    3. 2 tbsp lemon juice
    4. 2 tsp garam masala
    5. 1 tsp turmeric powder
    6. 1 tsp red chili powder
    7. 2 tbsp butter
    8. 1 cup tomato puree
    9. 1/2 cup heavy cream
    10. 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
    11. Salt to taste
    12. Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

    Instructions:

    1. In a bowl, combine yogurt, lemon juice, garam masala, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Add the chicken pieces and marinate for at least 1 hour.
    2. Heat butter in a pan over medium heat. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until fragrant.
    3. Add tomato puree and cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    4. Add the marinated chicken pieces to the pan and cook until they are fully cooked.
    5. Stir in the heavy cream and simmer for another 5 minutes.
    6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
    7. Enjoy your butter Chicken

  • Tandoori Chicken

    • courtesy:Love Deep

    Tandoori Chicken is a popular Indian dish made by marinating chicken in yogurt and spices, then cooking it in a tandoor or oven. It's known for its smoky flavor and tender texture, often served with raita or naan.

    Here's the recipe:

      Ingredients:

      1. Large pieces of skinless bony chicken, like thighs.
      2. 1 cup plain yogurt
      3. salt
      4. oil fats
      5. Tandoori Masala
      6. Red chili powder
      7. Kasuri Methi
      8. Chaat Masala

      Instructions:

      1. Prepare Marinade: Combine yogurt, lemon juice, tandoori masala powder, red chili powder, kasuri methi, and salt in a bowl. Add the chicken pieces and mix well to coat them evenly. Cover and marinate for at least 1 hour or overnight in the refrigerator.
      2. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the marinated chicken on a baking tray lined with parchment paper.
      3. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred at the edges.
      4. Remove from oven and let it cool slightly before serving. Serve with your favorite chutney or raita.
      5. Enjoy your Dish.
